SoFi Technologies

Ticker: SOFI | Founded 2011 | CEO: Anthony Noto | Bank Charter: Feb 2022

SoFi Technologies is a member-centric, one-stop shop for digital financial services designed to help members borrow, save, spend, invest, and protect their money. By operating as a vertically integrated digital bank with a national bank charter, SoFi eliminates traditional banking friction through a unified mobile experience.

vs. SoFi Advantage
Traditional Banks Fully digital one-stop shop vs disjointed legacy offerings; high APY vs near-zero; no account fees
Neo-Banks (Chime etc.) National bank charter enables funding loans with deposits at lower cost; fintechs without lending can't replicate high APY economics
